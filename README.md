**Mischief in Broland 🦝**

Broland exists and we're not pretending it doesn't.

We're not asking permission to be here.

We're not defined by them. We're just aware of the landscape.

And we're having more fun than they are.

**Hello there!**

A group of raccoons is called a mischief. Welcome to yours.

Mischief in Broland is a private community for people who work in tech and have noticed that it is, occasionally, completely feral out here. Not in a fun way. The kind where you finish a meeting and think "was that just me or was that genuinely unhinged" and you have nobody to ask.

This is somewhere to ask.

We are not a professional development space. We are not a networking opportunity. We are not here to help you optimise your personal brand or crush your Q3 goals.

We are raccoons. We live in the bin. We find treasure where others see trash. We look out for each other, we call out the bullshit, and we do not apologise for taking up space in a landscape that wasn't built for us.

---

**What we talk about**

Whether something that happened was actually as unhinged as it felt. Whether that bro is lying to you (yes, probably). What we made, what we read, what we watched, what keeps us sane outside of work. How to think through the thing you've been going in circles about for three days. And occasionally we just scream into the void together. That's valid too.


*You didn't stumble in here by accident. You're exactly where you're supposed to be.*
